About the gallery

Where art comes to life

Galerie Lumière is a contemporary art gallery in the heart of Brussels. We represent both established and emerging artists, presenting rotating exhibitions of photography, painting, and digital art throughout the year. Every work we show is chosen for its ability to provoke, move, and inspire.
